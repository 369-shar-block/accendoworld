# ACCENDO World — Editor Guide

Welcome! This guide walks you through everything you can do as an editor on the ACCENDO website.

---

## Getting started

### Signing in

1. Go to **https://accendoworld.com/login**
2. Enter the email and password that were set up for your account
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You'll be taken to the admin dashboard at **https://accendoworld.com/admin**

### Signing out

Click **Sign out** at the bottom of the left sidebar on any admin page.

---

## The dashboard

**URL:** https://accendoworld.com/admin

The dashboard shows four stats at a glance:

| Stat | What it means |
|------|--------------|
| **Total products** | Every product in the system (visible + hidden) |
| **Visible on site** | Products currently showing on the public website |
| **Bestsellers** | Products featured in the "Bestsellers" section on the homepage |
| **New arrivals** | Products featured in the "New Arrivals" horizontal scroll on the homepage |

From here you can jump to:

- **Manage products** — add, edit, or remove products
- **Manage reels** — upload the short Instagram videos shown on the homepage
- **Edit contact info** — update email, shipping text, social links, and more

## Managing products

**URL:** https://accendoworld.com/admin/products

This page lists every product in the catalogue. Each row shows the product image, title, category, and status badges (Hidden, Bestseller, New arrival).

### Searching

Use the search bar at the top to filter products by **title** or **category**. For example, type "clogs" to see all clogs, or "David" to see all David products.

### Adding a new product

1. Click the **+ Add product** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Product photo** — click the dashed box to choose an image file from your computer (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
   - **Title** — the product name shown on the website (e.g., "Pink Bow Clogs")
   - **Category** — pick from the dropdown or type a custom one. The category controls which filter tab the product appears under on the Collections page:
     - Must start with **"Men's"** to appear under the Men's tab
     - Must start with **"Women's"** to appear under the Women's tab
     - Must start with **"Kids'"** to appear under the Kids' tab
   - **Bestseller label** (optional) — if this product is marked as a bestseller, this overrides the category text shown on the homepage bestseller card (e.g., "Designer Collection" instead of "Women's Clogs")
   - **Visible on site** — checked by default. Uncheck to save a product as a draft without showing it publicly
   - **Bestseller** — check to feature this product in the Bestsellers section on the homepage
   - **New arrival** — check to feature this product in the New Arrivals horizontal scroll on the homepage
3. Click **Create product**
4. The product will appear on the public website within a few minutes

### Editing a product

1. Find the product in the list (use search if needed)
2. Click the **Edit** button on that row
3. Change any fields — title, category, bestseller label, or toggle the checkboxes
4. Click **Save**

> **Note:** You cannot change the product photo after uploading. To replace an image, delete the product and re-add it with the new photo.

### Deleting a product

1. Click the **Delete** button on the product row
2. Confirm the deletion when prompted
3. The product and its image are permanently removed

> **Warning:** Deletion cannot be undone. Make sure you want to remove the product before confirming.

### Hiding a product (without deleting)

If you want to temporarily remove a product from the public website without deleting it:

1. Click **Edit** on the product
2. Uncheck **Visible on site**
3. Click **Save**

The product stays in your admin list but won't appear on the public website. Check the box again to bring it back.

## Managing reels (Instagram videos)

**URL:** https://accendoworld.com/admin/reels

Reels are short vertical videos that show on the homepage in an "On Instagram" section. Each one plays automatically (with no sound), loops forever, and when a visitor taps it, it opens your Instagram — the Instagram **app** on phones, or Instagram in a **new browser tab** on computers.

### Before you start: set your Instagram handle

The reels need to know which Instagram account to open. Set this **once**:

1. Go to **Edit contact info** (https://accendoworld.com/admin/contact)
2. Find the **Instagram handle** field
3. Type just your username — for example `accendoworld` (no `@`, no full link)
4. Click **Save changes**

This same handle also powers the Instagram links in the footer and on the contact page, so you only set it in one place.

> The Reels page shows a banner at the top telling you whether your handle is set yet.

### Adding a reel

1. On the Reels page, click **+ Add reel**
2. **Vertical video (9:16)** — click the dashed box and choose your video file. Use a tall, phone-shaped video (the same shape as an Instagram Reel or Story). MP4 works best. The file must be **under 50 MB** (that is the storage limit). Around 30 seconds is a good length; that is a suggestion, not a limit, but shorter videos upload faster and load faster for visitors.
3. **Title** (optional) — a name just for your own reference; visitors may see it as a small caption
4. **Instagram post link** (optional) — paste the link to that specific reel on Instagram if you want the "open in a new tab" (desktop) to land on that exact post. Leave it blank to just open your profile.
5. **Cover image** (optional) — a still picture shown for a moment while the video loads. If you skip it, the video's first frame is used.
6. **Visible on site** — checked by default. Uncheck to save it without showing it yet.
7. Click **Add reel**

The reel appears on the homepage within a few minutes.

### Editing or removing a reel

- **Edit** lets you change the title, the Instagram post link, and whether it's visible. (To swap the actual video, delete the reel and add a new one.)
- **Delete** permanently removes the reel and its video. This cannot be undone.

### Tips

- Upload a few reels for the best look — they line up in a row visitors can swipe through.
- Videos should be **tall (vertical)**, not wide, or they'll look letterboxed.
- If you have no reels, the "On Instagram" section simply doesn't appear — it never shows an empty space.

## Editing contact info

**URL:** https://accendoworld.com/admin/contact

This page lets you edit all the text shown on the public Contact page and in the Footer across every page.

### Editable fields

| Field | Where it appears |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Contact email** | Contact page info card + Footer |
| **Shipping message** | Contact page info card ("Global shipping available worldwide") |
| **Response time** | Contact page info card ("We respond within 24–48 hours") |
| **Footer tagline** | The paragraph under "ACCENDO" in the footer |
| **Website URL label** | Shown as text in the footer |
| **Footer shipping note** | Short line in the footer contact section |
| **Location headline (line 1)** | Dark section on the contact page (e.g., "Based in India,") |
| **Location headline (line 2)** | Rendered in italics below line 1 (e.g., "Serving the World") |
| **Location description** | Body text under the location headline |
| **Instagram handle** | Your username only (e.g. `accendoworld`). Powers the Reels links **and** the Instagram buttons on the contact page + footer |
| **Instagram URL (optional)** | Only needed to override the link built from your handle. Leave as `#` to just use the handle |
| **Facebook URL** | Shown as a button on the contact page + link in the footer |

### How to edit

1. Change any field values in the form
2. Click **Save changes** at the bottom
3. Changes go live on the public website within a few minutes

### Social links

- **Instagram:** just set the **Instagram handle** (username only, e.g. `accendoworld`) — the site builds the full link for you everywhere. You only need the separate **Instagram URL** field if you want to point somewhere unusual.
- **Facebook:** paste the full URL (e.g., `https://www.facebook.com/accendoworld`)
- To **hide** a social link, leave the handle blank / set the URL to just `#`
- Social buttons only appear on the contact page and footer when they have a real URL

---

## How changes go live

After you save changes in the admin panel, they appear on the public website within **up to 1 hour** on the next visitor's request. This is because the website caches pages for performance.

To see your changes faster:

- **On your own browser:** Do a hard refresh (Ctrl + Shift + R on Windows, Cmd + Shift + R on Mac)
- **For all visitors:** Changes will show up automatically within the cache period — no action needed

## Troubleshooting

**I can't sign in**
- Double-check your email and password
- If you've forgotten your password, ask the site administrator to reset it in the Supabase dashboard

**My changes aren't showing on the website**
- Changes can take up to 1 hour to appear for all visitors
- Try a hard refresh in your browser (Ctrl + Shift + R)

**The image I uploaded looks wrong**
- Use high-quality **square images (1:1 aspect ratio, e.g. 1600×1600)** for best results — product cards now show the whole square photo without cropping the edges
- JPG, PNG, and WebP formats are supported
- There is no file size limit, but smaller files (under 2MB) load faster for visitors

**My reel looks squished or has black bars**
- Reels must be **vertical (tall, 9:16)** — the same shape as an Instagram Reel or Story
- A wide (landscape) video will show black bars on the sides

**I accidentally deleted a product**
- Deletions are permanent. You'll need to re-add the product with a new photo upload
